Services

Aamir A. Faruqui MD Inc. provides a variety of services to treat your sleep and pulmonary conditions

Offerings

    1. Sleep and pulmonary consultation specializing in sleep apnea, offering various treatment options
    2. Manage various sleep disorders and pulmonary conditions including asthma and COPD
    3. Provide home sleep testing
    4. Pulmonary function testing in collaboration with various labs in local areas
    5. Diagnostic and therapeutic procedures: bronchoscopy and thoracentesis

Speciality

Pulmonology

Pulmonologists treat ailments of the lungs and respiratory system, such as asthma, pneumonia, tuberculosis, complicated chest infections and chronic obstructive pulmonary diseases including emphysema.

Subspecialities

  • Asthma & COPD
  • Critical Care Medicine
  • Pleural Disease
  • Sleep Medicine
