About Dr. Faruqui

Dr. Faruqui is a pulmlnologist with over 20 years of experience. He has been the president of Aamir A Faruqui, MD Inc. since June of 2006. After receiving his MBBS from Aga Khan University in Pakistan in 1991, Dr. Faruqui moved to Illinois where he completed his fellowship in Pulmonary & Critical Care at the University of Illinois Chicago. Dr. Faruqui also completed a fellowship at Stanford University in Sleep Medicine in 2000, before receiving an MBA from the University of Chicago in 2015.
